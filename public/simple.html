The Problem

Your workers are living
paycheck to paycheck

Contract labour in Indian manufacturing faces a daily financial crisis that hurts their productivity — and yours.

30-Day Wait

Contract workers wait an entire month to access wages they earned weeks ago — creating acute cash stress and forcing dangerous decisions.

High Attrition

Financial distress is the #1 reason for absenteeism and contract labour turnover. Replacing a worker costs 2–3× their monthly wage.

Predatory Lending

Workers turn to informal moneylenders charging 40–120% interest. This debt trap reduces long-term loyalty and productivity.
